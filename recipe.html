Fresh Mango Smoothie Recipe

Ingredients

500ml of milk
1 liter of water
1 Mango
1.5 Banana
Ice cream (how much you want)

First you have to chop the bananas and put it into a blender. Then chop the strawberries and Mango. Then Add the mango and the strawberries into the same blender, Second if you like milk add the milk, otherwise add the water and Blend all for 1 minute. Finally put the mixture into a glass and put the ice cream into a galls cointainer.

If you want you can add 3 ice cubes. This smoothie is awesome when you are hungry and healthy with a lot of proteins.
