We must protect and save our planet

Did you know this about the plastic?

According to a recent report from International Coastal Cleanup 2019, Plastic occupies 4 places in the top 5 wastes found on the coasts.

What do we use plastic for?

Plastic is the most abundant and used element on a daily basis because of how incredibly useful it is. We use it to bottle water, to pvc pipes, to build tables, vases, and almost everything you can imagine.

How much plastic do we use?

We are producing over 380 million tons of plastic every year, and some reports indicate that up to 50% of that is for single-use purposes - utilized for just a few moments, but on the planet for at least several hundred years.

Why should we recycle plastic?

do you have an idea of how long it takes for plastics to degrade back into the environment? The answer may surprise you. It’s between 500-1000 years. This is well beyond comprehension for most people.

What can we do?

We need to recycle it! And here you find many ways to do it. We should do…

    • Create Recycled Plastic Bottle Supply Cups
    • Reuse Coffee Creamer Containers for Snack Storage
    • Make a DIY Plastic Bottle Planter
    • The most important: Try to not use plastic.
