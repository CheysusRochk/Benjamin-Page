ELon Musk´s Biography

Actually his name is known by everyone. Elon Musk was born in South Africa and he grew up in Pretoria. His mother Maye Musk is a model and his father Errol was a mechanical and electrical engineering. When he was child he seemed to understand things quicker than the other kids.

At about the time of his parents’ divorce, when he was 10, Musk developed an interest in computers. He taught himself how to program, and when he was 12 he sold his first software: a game he created called Blastar.

Later Musk launched his first company, Zip2 Corporation, in 1995 with his brother, Kimbal Musk. Then He sold the company for $307 million in cash and $34 million in stock options. With that money He just could found PayPal before (x.com) and in 2002, Elon earned his first billion when PayPal was acquired by Ebay. Since that moment he dedicated to his other companies like SpaceX, Tesla and SolarCity for the humanity development and to achieve the intergalactic dream.
